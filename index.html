Is your business ready to scale sustainably?

A short benchmark designed to help growing businesses identify operational bottlenecks, scalability gaps, and founder dependency risks.
As businesses grow, operational complexity often increases faster than expected.

This benchmark is designed to help founder-led businesses identify common scalability constraints before they begin materially impacting growth, delivery, or visibility.

Most businesses score between 4–7.

Growth Visibility
  • We have a repeatable lead generation process that does not rely entirely on referrals.
  • We can forecast future revenue with reasonable confidence.
  • Our ideal client profile and target market are clearly defined.

Founder Dependency
  • The business can continue operating smoothly without daily founder involvement.
  • Operational knowledge is documented and transferable across the team.
  • Client relationships are not dependent on a single individual.
3
Systems & Operations
  • Core systems and workflows are integrated rather than fragmented across tools.
  • Operational reporting provides clear visibility into business performance.
  • The business can scale delivery without proportionally increasing operational strain.
